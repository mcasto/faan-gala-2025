Lights, camera, action! Finally, the much-awaited sequel to the Gala FAAN-tastica is coming to Cuenca with Gala FAAN-TASTICA Old Hollywod!
On Novemeber 22, 2025, FAAN is continuing its beautiful friendship with dog lovers all over the world.
This year, our gala will once again be held at Estancia Rosario. It will focus on funding the new physical shelter located in Tarqui.
Our dogs have always depended on the kindness of strangers. Frankly, my dears, we DO Give a Damn about the shelter dog's welfare. This new shelter will provide our photogenic pups with an opportunity to improve their living conditions. They have been under the loving care of Jose, the leader of the pack, but it's time to be homeward-bound in a beautiful new facility while awaiting their forever homes.
They are ready for their close-ups, and we cannot wait.
Join us in this opportunity to provide a wonderful life for our charismatic canines.
